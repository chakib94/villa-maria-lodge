The Lodge

A Sanctuary of Nature & Comfort

Hidden in lush green and organic gardens, Villa Maria Lodge offers a tranquil and harmonious escape where nature takes centre stage. Set across a single level within landscaped surroundings, the lodge embraces a design philosophy that connects indoors with the natural world.

The property features five beautifully appointed guest rooms, each designed to provide comfort and serenity. Guests can enjoy use of the shared swimming pool, perfect for refreshing dips while admiring the panoramic views.

Surrounded by olive trees and aromatic herbs, Villa Maria Lodge invites you to slow down, breathe deeply, and reconnect with the simple pleasures of Mediterranean living.
